Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

Overview

Prompteka is a privacy-focused macOS application for organizing and managing AI prompts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete data ownership.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data. All information you create in Prompteka remains exclusively on your device.

Specifically:

  • All prompts, folders, and settings are stored locally in an SQLite database on your Mac
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind
  • No user accounts, authentication, or cloud services
  • No internet connection required for the app to function

Local Data Storage

Prompteka stores all application data in the following location on your Mac:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/com.prompteka.app/

This data includes:

  • Your prompt library (text content, titles, icons, colors)
  • Folder organization and hierarchy
  • Application settings and preferences
  • Window layout and display preferences

Data Export and Backup

Prompteka provides JSON export/import functionality that allows you to:

  • Create backups of your prompt library
  • Transfer data between devices manually
  • Restore your library if needed

These export files are created and managed entirely by you. We have no access to or knowledge of these files.

Third-Party Services

Prompteka does not integrate with or use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Children's Privacy

Prompteka does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since the app does not connect to the internet, you are responsible for checking this page periodically for updates.

Your Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at all times
  • Export: Use the Export feature to create a JSON backup
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app removes all local data, or you can manually delete specific prompts/folders
  • Transfer: Use Export/Import to move data between devices
